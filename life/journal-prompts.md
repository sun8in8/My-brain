# Journal Prompts

Prompts for reflection — use anytime, or pick one to inspire a video.

## Mindfulness & presence
- What am I carrying today that I could put down?
- Where in my body do I feel tension right now?
- What would I tell a friend who felt exactly how I feel today?

## Creativity & content
- What story am I afraid to tell — and why might that be the one I should?
- What do I wish someone had said to me five years ago?
- What does my audience most need to hear right now?

## Growth & reflection
- What am I most proud of this month?
- What would I do differently if I weren't afraid of being judged?
- What is working? What can I stop doing?

## Affirmations (tied to A–Z book)
- Which letter am I living today?
- What affirmation do I need to hear right now?
