# Script Library

All scripts live here. Name files clearly: `YYYY-MM-topic.md`

## Structure for every script
1. **Hook** — first 30 seconds. Why should they keep watching?
2. **Context** — set the scene or share a story
3. **Core content** — the teaching, story, or meditation
4. **Call to action** — subscribe, download cards, check the book

## Reusable hooks bank
_Add your best opening lines here as you find them_

- "Close your eyes… actually wait, keep them open just a little longer."
- "This story is for the part of you that's been carrying too much today."
- "Before we begin, take one slow breath with me…"
