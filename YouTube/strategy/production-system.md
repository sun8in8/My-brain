# Series 2 Production System
*Last updated: June 2026*

---

## The core rule
**1 episode per week. 4-episode buffer before going live.**

Consistency beats frequency. One episode every week for 6 months beats 3/week for 6 weeks then silence. The buffer protects you when life happens.

---

## Batch workflow — work in threes

| Week | Focus | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| Week A | Record | 3 episodes back-to-back in one session |
| Week B | Edit | Edit all 3 |
| Week C | Publish | Ep 1 goes live, prep 2 & 3 for scheduling |

One recording session per week — not three separate days.

---

## Episode pipeline

| Step | Owner | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Script finalised + personalised | Me | 30 min (week before recording) |
| Record (3 episodes) | Me | 2–3 hrs |
| Edit audio/video | Me | Per episode |
| Captions, title, description, social copy, email, ManyChat | AI | Once edit is done |
| Schedule & publish | Me | 15 min |

---

## Before every recording session

- Final script printed or on screen
- Triggers list confirmed
- Warm light set up (natural light iPhone setup)
- Ambient sound ready
- Door closed, phone on silent

Nothing else. AI handles everything after the voice is captured.

---

## The mailing list CTA (every episode, same wording)

> *"Reply QUIET and I'll send you the free guide."*

ManyChat handles delivery. Never changes. Never needs touching.

---

## The bottleneck to watch
Editing is the one time-heavy step that cannot be delegated yet. Protect that time. If editing is falling behind, slow publish cadence — do not slow recording.
