# Brand Bible — Shhh Storytime Starts

## Brand essence
Calm. Warm. Grounded. Nurturing.
A safe space where storytelling meets mindfulness and affirmations.

## Name & meaning
**Shhh Storytime Starts** — the "shhh" signals quiet, calm, and the invitation to slow down.
The name itself is a gentle instruction.

## Voice & tone
- Speaks like a trusted friend who happens to be a mindfulness teacher
- Warm, never clinical. Soft, never weak.
- Accessible — no jargon, no gatekeeping
- Sentences are short when calming. Slower when guiding.

## Audience
People who are tired, overwhelmed, or anxious — and curious about mindfulness but not sure where to start. Age range roughly 25–45. Often women. Often parents or professionals.

## Content pillars
1. ASMR storytelling
2. Affirmations (tied to A–Z book and free cards)
3. Guided meditations
4. Mindfulness for everyday life
